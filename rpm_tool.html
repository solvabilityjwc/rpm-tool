Indirect Rate Calculation
Fringe Rate  = Fringe Pool ÷ (Direct Labor + OH Labor + G&A Labor)
Overhead Rate = OH Pool ÷ (DL + Fringe on DL)
G&A Rate    = G&A Pool ÷ (DL + Direct Non-Labor + Fringe Pool + OH Pool)

These three rates feed directly into the Wrap Rate Calculator.
